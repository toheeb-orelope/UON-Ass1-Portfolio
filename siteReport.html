Module Overview

As a computing student, Web Development (CSY1063-STD) is overall my favourite module this semester due to the passion I have for programming. I gained a lot of inspiration from different resources such as Samson, a year 2 student of the University of Northampton, Real Time Color, Web Code Tools for my custom animation, and Box Shadow Property Tips for my box-shawod effects. These resources helped me sharpen my UI/UX project. Overall praise goes to my favourite Tutor for this semester for his tireless support both day and night, physically and virtually. My friends and I call him the comedic lecturer because the method of his delivery makes the overall journey very interesting, and no one would ever want to miss his class. Such a great tutor Manny Hanry is.

challenges

I faced a series of challenges. Firstly, I began my assessment immediately upon seeing the assessment brief, unaware of the rules and format we needed to base our design on, which included Semantic HTML and the use of Grid layout using CSS for our project to make it user-friendly and mobile-optimized. This was because I was building my website based on YouTube tutorials, which did not seem to adhere to industrial standards. Secondly, I had to start editing my project to meet the required format, making debugging a nightmare as I worked to make it mobile-responsive and undertake other necessary editing tasks.
